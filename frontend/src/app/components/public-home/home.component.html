Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using SwapHub.

Is SwapHub completely free to use?

Yes, SwapHub is completely free to use. We don't charge any fees for creating an account, listing items, or making exchanges.

How do I ensure a safe exchange?

We recommend meeting in public places, bringing a friend, and thoroughly checking items before completing the exchange. Our platform also has a rating system to help build trust between users.

What items are not allowed on SwapHub?

SwapHub prohibits illegal items, dangerous goods, counterfeit products, and certain categories like medications, alcohol, and tobacco.
